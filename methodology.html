My Methodology

Penetration Testing
  • Information Gathering – Identify targets and gather intelligence.
  • Vulnerability Analysis – Scan and enumerate vulnerabilities.
  • Exploitation – Simulate attacks to exploit weaknesses.
  • Post-Exploitation – Assess impact and escalate access.
  • Reporting – Provide detailed findings and remediation guidance.
Vulnerability Assessments
  • Asset Identification – Catalog all systems and applications.
  • Vulnerability Scanning – Automated and manual scans.
  • Risk Prioritization – Evaluate based on severity and exploitability.
  • Remediation Planning – Recommend fixes and mitigations.
  • Verification – Rescan to confirm vulnerabilities are resolved.
Network Security Audits
  • Network Mapping – Document network architecture and devices.
  • Configuration Review – Check firewalls, ACLs, and policies.
  • Penetration Testing – Test network defenses and segmentation.
  • Policy Compliance – Ensure alignment with standards (e.g., NIST, ISO).
  • Reporting & Recommendations – Actionable security improvements.
Social Engineering Tests
  • Reconnaissance – Gather employee info and organizational data.
  • Phishing Campaigns – Design and launch controlled email attacks.
  • Physical Tests – Attempt unauthorized physical access (if authorized).
  • Training Recommendations – Educate staff based on results.
  • Detailed Reporting – Document findings and risks.
Red Team Operations
  • Objective Definition – Align on goals and scope.
  • Recon & Planning – Intelligence gathering and attack strategy.
  • Execution – Multi-vector simulated attacks.
  • Post-Op Analysis – Assess success and gaps.
  • Comprehensive Reporting – Executive and technical reports.
Incident Response & Forensics
  • Detection & Identification – Confirm breach and scope.
  • Containment – Stop ongoing damage and isolate affected systems.
  • Eradication – Remove threats and vulnerabilities.
  • Recovery – Restore systems and services.
  • Lessons Learned – Post-incident review and improvements.
