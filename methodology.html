How I Work

  1. Initial Recon & Scoping: Understanding your systems, goals, and defining the scope of work.
  2. Vulnerability Scanning: Using automated and manual tools to identify weaknesses.
  3. Exploitation & Penetration: Ethical attempts to exploit vulnerabilities to assess real risks.
  4. Reporting & Recommendations: Clear, actionable reports detailing findings and fixes.
  5. Follow-up & Retesting: Confirming vulnerabilities have been resolved and verifying security posture.
